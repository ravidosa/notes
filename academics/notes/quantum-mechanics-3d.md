	$-\frac{\hbar}{2m}\nabla^2\Psi(\vec{r},t)+U(\vec{r},t)\Psi(\vec{r},t)=i\hbar\frac{\partial}{\partial t}\Psi(\vec{r},t)$
	time independent: $-\frac{\hbar}{2m}\nabla^2\psi(\vec{r})+U(\vec{r})\psi(\vec{r})=E\psi(\vec{r})$
